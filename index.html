PROJECT



 

ABSTRACT


An EV Battery Charger with Power Factor Corrected Bridgeless Zeta Single phase ac-dc PFC converters have received much attention due to the dramatic growth in the use of electronic equipment and these ac-dc converters introduce harmonic currents. These harmonic currents cause a lower power factor at the ac mains, besides voltage distortion and noise. Converter Topology implemented for electric vehicle charging system. This converter provides the high power factor correction It can charge the battery fast as it can provide the stable output.




 

The zeta converter is operated at the continuous and discontinues conduction modes. To comply with harmonic standards and to increase transmission efficiency in power systems, conventional ac-dc converter designs cannot deliver high efficiency over a wide operation range in both step-up and step down voltage conversions. A new topology design that improves efficiency and reduces the complexity of the control is attempted in this work.


OBJECTIVE


The objective of the project is to design of zeta converter for electrical vehicle charging system.Constant voltage and constant current is maintained for electric vehicle charging system.The zeta converter provides the fast vehicle charging system, as compared to other converters.
